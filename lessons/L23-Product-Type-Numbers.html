💡 Tip: When ordering replacement parts, always use the exact product type number. The suffix (e.g., -BA1, -BB1) indicates the specific revision and variant. Check the label on the physical card for confirmation.

📝 Knowledge Check

Q1: What is the product type number for the MD Plus Controller?

A: KJ2003X1-BB1

Q2: Which product type number corresponds to the Series 2 AI, 16-Channel, 4–20 mA HART card?

A: KJ3223X1-BA1

Q3: What are the two VerticalPLUS carrier types for power/controller mounting?

A: KJ4003X1-BC1 (4-Wide Power/Controller Carrier) and KJ4003X1-BG1 (4-Wide Power Carrier)
