📝 Glossary Quick Check

Q1: What does "LocalBus" refer to in a DeltaV system?

A: The combination of a Power/Controller Carrier, its associated I/O Interface Carriers, and the cables that connect them.

Q2: What is the difference between AWG 14 and AWG 000 wire?

A: AWG 14 has a cross-sectional area of 2.08 mm², while AWG 000 has 85.02 mm². Smaller AWG numbers indicate larger wire.

Q3: What does "Intrinsically Safe" mean in the context of DeltaV field circuits?

A: Circuits designed to limit energy to field devices below the ignition threshold for hazardous materials, under both normal and fault conditions.
